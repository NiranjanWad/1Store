About Us

We are on a mission to get millions of merchants both in organised and unorganised sector to be a part of the digital payments ecosystem. Our Pay service is all about social payments. It is an engaging and secure way for you to seamlessly Pay to friends, family, and merchants in less than 5 seconds. It also enables merchants, small or large to accept digital payments in less than 1 minute after registering on the 1Store App.
